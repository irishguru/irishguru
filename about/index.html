Architects and engineers who deliver outcome-tied results

We founded Irish Guru to deliver engineering excellence for organisations in Ireland and the UK. We reject generic templates, open-ended hour sheets, and compliance theatricals.

Experienced Practitioners On-Site

Irish Guru is a specialized consulting enterprise headquartered in Dublin, with on-site deployment capacity throughout the United Kingdom. We work closely with CTOs, Engineering directors, and security sponsors who require real delivery on complex, regulated transformation programs.

We scope each engagement to distinct targets: cloud cost thresholds minimized, release velocities doubled, or audit findings resolved. We do not sell junior body leasing — we embed veteran architects who operate inside your codebase, lead daily stand-ups, and stay on-site through production go-lives.
