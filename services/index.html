Architecture to production — nothing outsourced.

Seven practice areas. Each led by a principal consultant who writes code, owns architecture decisions, and delivers production milestones — not reports.

Practice 01

Artificial Intelligence

We design and deliver production AI systems — not proof-of-concepts that stall before deployment. Every engagement ends with a model in production, governed, monitored, and handed over to your team.

  • Use-case validation and business-case development for AI programmes
  • LLM integration with RAG pipelines and vector database design
  • MLflow-based model registry, versioning, and drift detection
  • Human-in-the-loop controls and model governance frameworks
  • EU AI Act compliance assessment and documentation
  • Azure OpenAI, Databricks, and open-source model deployment
  • Automated data pipelines for ML feature stores and training datasets

Typical Outcome

First governed AI model in regulated production environment: 14 weeks from kickoff.

Practice 02

Cloud Enablement

We design and build cloud landing zones, execute migration programmes, and establish FinOps disciplines for Irish and UK organisations — from first workload to multi-cloud at scale.

  • Multi-cloud landing zone design and implementation (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Well-Architected reviews and remediation sprints
  • Lift-and-shift and re-platform migration programme delivery
  • FinOps programme establishment with tagging policy and showback reporting
  • Data residency and sovereignty baseline design for EU and UK requirements
  • Cloud disaster recovery and multi-region failover architecture
  • Network connectivity and private peering across hybrid environments

Typical Outcome

25–35% cloud infrastructure cost reduction within two quarters of FinOps implementation.

Practice 03

DevOps & DevSecOps

We design, build, and hand over CI/CD pipelines with embedded security controls. Engineering teams deliver more often and with less risk — without a dedicated security team for each pipeline.

  • CI/CD pipeline design and implementation on GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Azure DevOps
  • Infrastructure as Code with Terraform, Pulumi, and Bicep
  • SAST, DAST, and SCA scanning integrated into automated gates
  • Container security, image signing, and supply-chain hardening
  • Secrets management with HashiCorp Vault and cloud-native KMS
  • FCA and NCSC DORA-aligned change control implementation
  • SLI, SLO, and error budget frameworks with Prometheus and Grafana

Typical Outcome

CVE patches deployed within 48 hours. Deployment frequency increases 3–4× in the first quarter.

Practice 04

Platform Engineering

We design and operate internal developer platforms that eliminate infrastructure friction — so engineering teams ship features instead of waiting for environments.

  • Internal developer portal design and Backstage implementation
  • Kubernetes fleet management and multi-tenant cluster design
  • Golden-path templates and self-service environment provisioning
  • GitOps delivery with ArgoCD and Flux
  • Service mesh deployment with Istio and Linkerd
  • Platform observability with Datadog, OpenTelemetry, and Loki
  • SRE practices, on-call framework design, and runbook development

Typical Outcome

Developer onboarding from weeks to hours. Infrastructure support tickets reduced by 65%.

Practice 05

Data & Analytics

We build data platforms that support both operational reporting today and AI workloads tomorrow — on a modern lakehouse foundation with proper governance from the start.

  • Lakehouse design on Databricks, Snowflake, and Azure Synapse
  • dbt modelling and data transformation layer design
  • Streaming data pipelines with Kafka and Azure Event Hubs
  • Data governance and cataloguing with Unity Catalog and Purview
  • Apache Airflow orchestration design and implementation
  • BI layer integration with Power BI and Tableau
  • GDPR-compliant data anonymisation and access control frameworks

Typical Outcome

Single governed data platform replacing three or more siloed tools within six months.

  • Architecture roadmap development with delivery phasing
  • Build vs buy analysis for platform and tooling decisions
  • Vendor selection and procurement support
  • Technology investment case development for CFO and board presentation
  • Engineering culture and operating model assessment
  • CTO office advisory and interim CTO support

Board-approved technology roadmap with phased delivery plan within six weeks of engagement start.
