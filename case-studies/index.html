Financial Services · Ireland

AI regulatory document extraction for an Irish bank

The challenge

The bank's compliance division manually classified over 12,000 regulatory files each quarter. Processing averaged 19 business days per batch, running backlog risk and tying highly-compensated risk analysts to repetitive file sorting tasks.

Our approach

Irish Guru designed and deployed an automated classification, parsing, and extract pipeline integrated directly with the bank's case records. We designed Human-in-the-Loop exception triggers, configured audit trail telemetry, and wrote model documentation approved by internal risk review.

Public Sector · United Kingdom

Multi-application cloud migration for a UK agency

The challenge

A UK agency managed over 100 applications across three end-of-life datacentres. Leadership required a migration path that guaranteed continuous service delivery and met strict NCSC security baselines throughout transition.

Our approach

We designed an Azure landing zone, migrated 83 workloads using clean containerized designs, and trained local operators on continuous integration systems. We established FinOps cost-monitoring and threshold alert protocols from week one.
